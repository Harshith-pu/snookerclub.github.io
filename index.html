Tables

3 Types of Tables are available in our club .

FRENCH

A Game played with three balls (two white and one red) on a table without pockets, in which the object is to drive one of the white balls (cue ball) into both of the other balls.

SNOOKER

Snooker is played on an English billiards table using 15 red, 6 coloured and one white cue ball. Points are scored by pocketing balls and forcing an opponent to give away points through 'snookers'. It may be played by two persons, pairs or a team..

8 POOL

Eight ball, also called stripes and solids, popular American pocket-billiards game in which 15 balls numbered consecutively and a white cue ball are used. Those numbered 1–7 are solid colours 9–15 are white with a single thick stripe in varying colours and the eight ball is black.
